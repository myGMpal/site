1 About This App

myGM Pal is an independent, fan-made companion app designed to help players plan and optimise their experience in WWE 2K myGM mode. The App provides tools including a roster tracker, match rating calculator, rivalry planner, and season planning features.

The App is developed and maintained by an individual developer based in Western Australia, Australia. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by World Wrestling Entertainment, Inc. (WWE), Take-Two Interactive Software, Inc., 2K Games, or Visual Concepts.

2 Information We Collect

Information you enter directly

The App allows you to manually enter roster information, match data, rivalry details, and season planning notes. This information is entered voluntarily and is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit this data to any server, and we cannot access it.

Information we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or any contact details
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Device contacts or calendar information
  • Browsing history or usage data from other apps
  • Any form of account or login credentials

Automatically collected technical information

The App itself does not automatically collect any analytics or usage data. However, the operating system platforms (Google Play / Apple App Store) may collect crash reports and basic performance data in accordance with their own privacy policies, which are separate from this policy.

Current Status

In the current version of myGM Pal (v1.0), no advertising SDKs are integrated and no personal data is collected by the App. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an in-app notice prior to the update taking effect.

3 How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into myGM Pal — including roster details, rivalry records, match history, and season plans — is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in local storage mechanisms.

  • Local only: Your data never leaves your device and is not backed up to any cloud server operated by us.
  • No account required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App or using the "Clear all data" option in the App's Settings screen. This permanently removes all locally stored information.
  • Device backups: If your device's operating system performs automatic backups (e.g. iCloud on iOS or Google Drive backup on Android), App data may be included in those device-level backups, which are governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

4 Advertising & Third-Party Services

Current version (v1.0)

The current version of myGM Pal contains no third-party advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking frameworks. No data is shared with any third party by the App.

We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using a device passcode or biometric lock to protect locally stored App data.

In the event that a future version of the App introduces any server-side data storage, we will implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest, and will update this policy accordingly.
